Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2025

About Cookies

When you visit our home buyer readiness assessment platform, small text files called cookies are stored on your device. These cookies play a crucial role in enhancing your assessment experience by remembering your preferences and improving the overall functionality of our services.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Required for core functionality such as navigating through your assessment and accessing secure areas of your account.

Analytical Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our assessment tools, enabling us to improve the user experience.

Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences to provide a personalized assessment experience.

Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant content about home buying resources and evaluate the effectiveness of our recommendations.

Consent

By continuing to use SaleHomeLand's assessment services, you consent to our cookie usage. While you can adjust cookie settings through your browser, please note that blocking certain cookies may affect your ability to access some features of our assessment platform.

Managing Your Cookies

You have full control over your cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, please be aware that disabling certain cookies may limit your access to some features of our home buyer readiness assessment tools.

Data Privacy and Security

We implement robust security measures to protect any personal data collected through cookies during your assessment process. This information is never shared inappropriately or used for purposes outside of improving your home-buying readiness experience.

Your Rights

In compliance with GDPR and CCPA regulations, you have the right to:

  • Request information about cookies stored during your assessment process
  • Request corrections or removal of cookie-related data
  • Opt out of non-essential cookies while using our services

Policy Revisions

This Cookie Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements affecting our assessment services. You will be notified of any significant changes to how we use cookies on our platform.
